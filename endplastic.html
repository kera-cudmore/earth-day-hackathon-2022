Charity Earth

Charities working withiin the theme of Ending Plastic Pollution.

England

PLASTIC FREE COMMUNITIES

"We’re a community network tackling single-use plastic from beaches & green spaces all the way back to the brands and businesses who create it."


JUST ONE OCEAN

“Our mission is to protect the ocean for future generations through science, education and communication."

Ireland

SEAL RESCUE IRELAND

"Seal Rescue Ireland is a registered charity dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation and release of sick, injured and orphaned seals from across the coast of Ireland. We actively engage the public in marine conservation through education, research and community outreach programmes."


SICK OF PLASTIC IRELAND

"Sick of Plastic is working to empower communities to pressure industry and decision makers to take a stand against plastic pollution."

Northern Ireland

LIVE HERE LOVE HERE

"At Live Here Love Here, we are passionate about creating a healthier and greener Northern Ireland which not only affects our physical health but comes with overwhelming social benefits. Through Live Here Love Here we can all do one small thing to help to make a big difference in our communities."


RECYCLE NOW NI

"Recycle Now NI provides advisory support and strategic campaign leadership through the development and delivery of campaigns."

Scotland

FRIENDS OF THE EARTH SCOTLAND

"Friends of the Earth work in Scotland for socially just solutions to environmental problems. We campaign to end environmental degradation and to create a green economy and a society which cherishes and protects the natural world. We think globally and act locally, enabling people to take individual and collective action."


ZERO WASTE SCOTLAND

"Zero Waste Scotland exists to lead Scotland to use products and resources responsibly, focusing on where we can have the greatest impact on climate change."

Wales

KEEP WALES TIDY

"‘We’re Keep Wales Tidy – the charity working across Wales to protect our environment for now and for the future. We have a collective vision of a beautiful Wales cared for and enjoyed by everybody."


AFONYDD CYMRU

"Afonydd Cymru was formed in 2008 as an umbrella organisation to represent member Rivers Trusts across Wales."
